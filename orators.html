Visualise and Rise

Event Details.

Round 1: Unblock  A team of two. Make your partner guess the word in BLOCK without using the given 3 words and use maximum of 4 words otherwise. Round 2: Blink and Sink  Watch the video very carefully and answer the questions.  Round 3: Mindkraft Giving a name of product along with four random words, they have to use those words and make a short maximum 4 line advertisement for it.

Sync your skills

Event Details.

Round 1: Guess What A pack of cards will be given to them to make a structure along with guessing the songs simultaneously. One should enact the song name given to them. In the end the structure is better in terms of height and how many they have guessed, the teams going to the next round will be decided.  Round 2: Twist Therapy Bring out all the hidden sides you have, showcase all the roles you can! A situation will be given and the judge will ask you to switch from one mood to another for the same situation. Round 3: Build Play win  Running puzzle! The puzzle wont be given but all the words to be made to complete that puzzle will be given.

Event Details.

Round 1: Black out A team of two one blind folded and the other not. In a room things are scattered based on different genre like education(books,pens, pencils etc), sports( bat,ball,racket etc) and the one not blind folded is allowed to look from a specified distance and guide his other partner the blind folded one to collect as many as possible.  Round 2: Petitioner Get our petition signed up by maximum people, the one who persuades goes to the next round.
