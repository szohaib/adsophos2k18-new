ROBOLYMPIA 2017




Robotics Centre , MJCET exists to connect students,mentors and schools in every community to a variety of successful and engaging technology based events,projects and competitions. Robolympia 2K17 brings in various teams around the city to compete head to head on a special playing field with robots they have designed,built and programmed. It is the amalgamation of technology and humanities for the betterment of human race. Gather all the wires and metals, for this is your chance to build awesome robots and test your mettle against enthusiasts who eat, sleep and breathe robotics.

RoboSoccer

Event Details.

If you are a Soccer freak & Robotics freak, then this is the best place for you! Robosoccer brings the thrill, excitement & amusement inspired by the beautiful game of Football. It helps us to learn about the importance of teamwork, passion, dealing with failure & having a sense of humour at the end of the day. Teams should build a manual robot for competing in a one-on-one Robosoccer tournament. Your defensive & attacking skills will be tested.

All Terrain

Event Details.

All Terrain is the competition in which the contestants are required to build a robot such that it can be raced through an uneven track. The challenges are to make your robot skid on a muddy surface, make it go uphill, through rough surface & much more. The one who overcomes the hurdles in shortest period of time will be the winner! So are you up for the challenge ?

Formula 1

Event Details.

Can you race a robot with maximum speed? Can you also control your robot accurately? If yes, then unleash your robots on the track & let the race begin! Formula-1 is a simple competition where couple of robots compete with each other to reach maximum distance in shortest possible time. The aim is to have more speed, more power with minimum time. Enrol, have fun & earn accolades.

Robo Sumo

Event Details.

Robot-sumo is a sport in which two robots attempt to push each other out of a circle. The aim is to built a robot which could be strong enough to tackle the opposition & knock them out of the arena. This is the best place to showcase your robotics talent. The one who knocks the other out will be declared as winner and will be advanced to next rounds.

Maze Runner

Event Details.

RoboMaze is a network of paths and hedges designed as a puzzle through which one has to find a way through a Robot. The participants are required to make their own robot for solving the puzzle in the shortest period of time. The challenge here is to find the shortest path to solve the puzzle. Your patience, ability to understand the surroundings & critical thinking skills will be tested here. So enrol and unlock your potential!
