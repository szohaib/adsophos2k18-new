Mind Maze

Event Details.

Round 1 - The team will be asked 20 questions in one minute. The team which gives the highest number of correct answers will qualify for the next round.
Round 2 - The team members will be given a program code filled with errors, they must debug it to move to the next round.
Round 3 - One team member with from each team will be given headphones to eliminate the outside noise. A question will be asked to the other member(without headphone) who must explain the answer to his/her team member(with headphones).
