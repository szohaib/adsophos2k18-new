Mechnova

Event Details.

The event will comprise of 5 teams and each team will have two members participating at a time in which the teams will compete against one another to reach the goal and claim the winning position.
Round 1: Each team will be provided with a box. The boxes shall have a regular household tool whichthey have to identify blindly by using only one of their hands. The teams will be allotted 2 minutes to do so. The top 3 teams with the correct answers in the shortest time will proceed to the next round.
Round 2: The qualified teams will be shown different parts of a variety of vehicles and asked to name the parts and identify their function. The team with the most number of correct answers will be declared the winner.
Benefits of the event:
Round 1: Participants will get acquainted with standard tools and their application upon discovery. It will be a test of practical knowledge and distinguishing ability.
Round 2: Participants will improve their retaining skills as they are encountered with the parts of vehicles. It will be a test of automobile knowledge.

Mr. Weld

Event Details.

This is a competition in Metal Arc Welding. It comprises of two rounds. They are as follows:
Round 1: The participants are asked to form a Bead Weld. The top 10 participants with the best beads will be advanced to the further round.

Technoledge

Event Details.

The event will comprise of 4 teams and each team will have two members participating at a time from which one will be the winner. Round 1:In this round the teams will be provided with house wiring components. A circuit diagram will be on the board. The 2 teams that completes the circuit connection correctly without any mistake in the shortest time will proceed to the next round.
Round 2: The remaining participants will be shown either a flowchart or an animation or short video clips of a process and will be asked to identify the name of the process.
E.g.: 1. An animation of working of a 4-stroke/2-stroke engine will be shown and the participants will be asked to identify the type of the engine. 2. A video clip of any operation on lathe will be shown and will be asked to identify the name of that particular operation. 3. A flowchart of steps involved in investment casting will be projected and will be asked to identify the name of the casting process. 4. A short video clip of working of a particular pump or turbine will be shown and the participants will be asked to identify the type of turbine or pump.
