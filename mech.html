Mechanova

Event Details.

The event will comprise of 4 teams and each team will have two members participating at a time in which the teams will compete against each other to reach the goal and claim the winning position.
Round 1: Each team will be provided with a box. The boxes shall have an everyday tool that they have to identify blindly by using their hands. The teams will be allotted 2 minutes. The top 3 teams with the correct answer in the shortest time will proceed to the next round.
Round 2: Each team will be given parts of an appliance in a dismantled manner and will be asked to assemble them as early as possible. The team which completes the assembly in the least time will be the winner.

Mechmerize

Event Details.

The event will comprise of 4 teams and each team will have two members participating at a time in which the top team claims the winning position.
Round 1: In this round the teams will be given an equation that they have to solve by stepping on a calculator drawn on the floor accordingly. The two teams that take minimum time to solve the given problem will progress to the final round. The equation may comprise of simple mathematical calculations.
Round 2: The two qualified teams shall be provided with house wiring components. A circuit diagram will be drawn on the board. The team that completes the circuit connection correctly within the shortest time will be declared as the winner.

Mr Weld

Event Details.

Description: This is a competition in Metal Arc Welding. It comprises of two rounds. They are as follows:
Round 1: The participants are asked to form a Bead Weld. The top 10 participants with the best beads will be advanced to the further round.
Round 2: Now they are asked to perform a Lap joint or T-Joint by arc welding. All the jobs will be inspected by Dr. Mohd. Viquar Mohiuddin Sir (Associate HOD, MED), the best two jobs among all the participants will be awarded with 1st and 2nd prizes respectively.

Technoledge

Event Details.

The event will comprise of 4 teams and each team will have two members participating at a time from which one will be the winner.
Round 1: The first round is basically a quiz about the history, new modifications, new softwares, logos, taglines, etc. of various Multi-National Companies(MNCs) like automobile companies GMC, Toyota, Chevrolet, etc. and software companies like Wipro, Microsoft, Yahoo, Google, etc. Points will be awarded accordingly to the teams who give the correct answers. The difficulty level of questions increasesfor every successive question. The top 2 teams with more points will advance to the next round.
Round 2: The remaining participants will be shown either a flowchart or an animation or short video clips of a process and will be asked to identify the name of the process.
